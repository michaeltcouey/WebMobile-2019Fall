Ducati Monster

Production 1993 - present

Class: Naked Bike

The Ducati Monster was originally designed by Miguel Angel Galluzzi and produced by Ducati in 1993.

The Monster line has had many different models. Often times a newer, more powerful model, would replace a previous model. In order to keep the cost of the bikes low, they were designed by using parts from existing Ducati models.
